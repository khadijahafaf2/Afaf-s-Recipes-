Easy Chocolate Cake Recipe!

Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour
  • Sugar
  • Unsweetened Cocoa powder
  • Baking powder
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Expresso powder
  • Milk
  • Vegetable oil
  • Vanilla extract
  • Boiling water

Instructions:

  1. Prep:Preheat oven to 350°F. Prepare two 9 inch cake pans spraying with baking spray or buttering.
  2. Whisk dry ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda,expresso powder and salt. Mix well.
  3. Mix wet ingredients: Add milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract. Make sure to Mix it on medium speed,until well combined.
  4. Added boiling water: Added boiling water to the batter and mix carefully until smooth.
  5. Bake: Pour the batter evenly into the pans. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Cool & Frost: Let cakes cool for 10 minutes, remove from pans and cool completey. Frost with chocolate buttercream frosting.

Enjoy your homemade chocolate cake! 🎂
